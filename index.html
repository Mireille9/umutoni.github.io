Welcome to Palliative Care Association Rwanda(PCAR)


The Palliative Care Association of Rwanda (PCAR)

is non-profit, non-governmental organization

devoted to improving awareness and access to

palliative care services throughout the country.

Discover More

Our mission


To ensure the promotion and provision

of acceptable, accessible and affordable,

quality palliative care throughout Rwanda.

OUR COUSES

Palliative care Association of Rwanda is a legal entity and have Memorandum of Understanding with the ministry of health, a member to African Palliative Care Association (APCA).

Advocacy

PCAR has contributed to the development of Rwanda National palliative care stand alone policy and availability Liquid morphine

Capacity Building

In partnership with the MOH, PCAR has supported training of trainers (TOTs) of health professionals in referral hospitals and District hospitals throughout the country.

Service

We endeavor to be the best in helping patients by visiting them in various hospitals and home.

Networking

PCAR does networking with different stakeholders as a strategy of joining together different efforts for raising awareness on palliative care at all levels.

OUR Vision

Quality palliative care for all in need.

Objectives

To create awareness about the needs and care of those with life threatening illnesses

To promote training and education, capacity building and research in palliative care across Rwanda

To advocate for recognition of palliative care as key component of health care and as the one of essential elements of primary health care

To promote harmonization of palliative care and standards hospices and palliative care providers

To promote partnership and networking with the GOR and other stakeholders.

To facilitate the development of palliative care program

To establish centers of excellence for palliative care at community level.
